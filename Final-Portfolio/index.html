About-me

Hey. I’m Natan. Not the best at writing about myself, but here goes.

I like messing with code. It started with curiosity — I typed some stuff, the screen changed, and that blew my mind a little. Been hooked ever since. I’m not some coding wizard or anything, but I enjoy learning, breaking things, and figuring out how to fix them. Sometimes it works on the first try (rare), sometimes I want to throw my laptop out the window (less rare), but I keep going. I’m currently studying Software Development and building small projects as I learn. Still figuring out what I enjoy most — front-end, back-end, design, whatever — but I know I like seeing things I made come to life. I don’t have a stack of certificates or a fancy résumé (yet), but I’ve got curiosity, and that’s been enough to keep me moving. This portfolio is simple, but it’s mine. And just like me, it’s a work in progress.
