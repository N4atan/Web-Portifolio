Hello, I’m Natan, a Fullstack Development student.

I’m not someone who has all the answers — but I try. I try to understand, to solve my problems, even when it feels like I’m moving in circles. My personality is shaped by curiosity, reflection, and persistence, even when I doubt myself. I’ve spent time exploring not just my strengths, but also my fears and contradictions. I understand I don’t need to be perfect — I just need to keep going, to keep trying. What motivates me is the process itself: trying to make sense of things, trying to build, trying to become better. I’m often caught between overthinking and acting, but I’m learning to trust that small steps matter. I value authenticity and depth. I’m not interested in looking like I have it all together; I’d rather be honest about where I’m at — someone in progress. This website is a reflection of that: my journey, my experiments, my attempts. Not a finished product, but a work in motion.
